Continuing Buisness

Reserve Drivers

Reserve Drivers are not used often enough in races through out the season. Last time, we covered possible solutions for this problem. However, due to the lack of reasonable and implementable ideas, we will hold off on this topic until next meeting. Everyone, please continue to search for said solutions. Please ask your fellow reserve drivers for input.

New Buisness

New Car Regulations

Regulations for 2023 for Formula 1 cars have changed yet again. The highlights of the changes include: Increased size of the front wing for better aerodynamics, Changes in the DRS activation, the new threshold being a 0.500 second gap between the car in front to gain DRS, and finally, we have decremented the radius of the tire rims by 5mm.

Cost Cap Changes

Changes in the cost cap for the Constructors are that we will decrease the cost cap around the board by $100k but to compensate for the losses, we will allow a longer access to wind tunnels across the board by 20 hours.

Snacks For The Meetings

It is to my absolute dissatisfaction and horror to discover that Corn Nuts are being served as snacks for this meetings. This is needs to be changed at once for I refuse to place another Ranch flavored Corn Nut in my mouth again. I suggest we switch to Oreos or King-Size Snickers, because I am not me when I am hungry.
